Pie Chart:

Bellow you can see a Pie chart and Table that was created from data extracted from a google sheet. Which shows how many fast food ad's are seen by children during 2012.

What Do These Results Show?

The results shown in this pie chart clearly show that a large number of fast food advertisements are seen by children (aged 6-11) This could be the reason why obesity is on the rise over the last decade, the rate of obesity in US adults has risen to one third of the population (36.5%), I think this is due to many children being shown fast food advertisments. I found that the reason why McDonalds advertisments are so far in the lead is beacuse McDonalds has the highest advertisment budget in the world. In 2012 mcdonalds spent 963 million US dollars on adverts, this means that for every 6 US dollars spent on advertisments one of them was from McDonalds. Another reason why McDonalds is on the top of fast food advertisements may be as they place their ad's in strategic places, like on highways, subways, on the television, and even at sporting events, all these places are strategic as they are in places where lots of people will see them. In conclusion, the reason why McDonalds is so far ahead of any other fast food advertisment is due to a high budget and strategic placement of adverts.
